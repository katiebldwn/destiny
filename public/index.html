How it Works

Destiny Search requires only two things: a username and an account type. It then conects to Bungie.net through a series of service endpoints to retrieve various pieces of publicly available character data. The result is a clean, concise view of a player's Destiny characters. Try it out.

Using Destiny Search on Your Site

Feel free to add links to Destiny Search results on your own site. Direct links to specific user results are simply:

http://www.destinyrep.com/#un=[username]&t=[accountType]

[username]
a psn username or xbox gamertag with all spaces removed
[accountType]
a 1 for xbox or 2 for psn

Please do let me know when the links go live or if you have any questions, but that's about all there is to it.

Privacy Policy

DestinyRep.com does not store any information about you or your Destiny account. Each time a search is performed, the data is pulled straight from Bungie.net. The data is not used in any fashion other than being shown as search results to users of the site.
